V1.0  ·  HOW TO USE
📅 Calendar

Tap any date to open its log. Enter your body weight, toggle gym attendance, and pick a muscle group tag. Teal cells show gym days. Stats strip shows total sessions, streak, and today's weight.

🏷️ Muscle Groups

Inside the day modal, type a name and tap ADD to create a custom muscle group tag. Tap a tag to select it for that day. Tap × to permanently delete a tag from all days. Tags auto-appear as Routines tabs.

📋 Routines

Each muscle group gets its own tab. Write exercises, sets, reps, and notes in the text area, then tap Save Routine. Plans are stored on-device for quick gym reference.

📈 Progress

Body weight chart plots your last 30 logged days. Below that, view all entries in reverse order. Add exercises by name to track 10-rep weights over time — each exercise gets its own mini chart.

📲 Install

Android: browser menu → Add to Home Screen. iOS Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen. Works fully offline after first load.
